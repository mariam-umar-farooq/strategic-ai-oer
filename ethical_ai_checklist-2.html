Ethical AI Checklist

Run any AI tool through these 8 questions before adopting it for your venture. Your responses are yours alone — nothing is shared or stored.

Your progress 0 / 8
01
Data Retention
I know what data this tool collects, how it is stored, and how long it is retained.
02
GDPR Compliance
This tool is GDPR-compliant, or I have confirmed it is safe to use in the EU context.
03
Beneficiary Data
I will not input identifiable beneficiary or client information into this tool without explicit consent and legal basis.
04
Bias Check
I have reviewed sample outputs for cultural assumptions, language bias, or blind spots relevant to my community.
05
Human Review
There is always a human reviewing AI outputs before they reach beneficiaries, donors, or the public.
06
Mission Test
Using this tool does not replace a human interaction where the relationship itself is the value.
07
Fallback Plan
If this tool became unavailable tomorrow, I have a way to continue the work without it.
08
Team Awareness
The people using this tool understand its limitations — not just its capabilities.
